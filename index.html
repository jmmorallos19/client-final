~~ About us ~~

Mission

To provide exceptional hospitality and memorable experiences by delivering top-notch accommodations, personalized services, and a variety of recreational activities. We are committed to promoting sustainable tourism practices that protect and enhance our natural surroundings, while positively impacting the local community.

Vision

To be the leading private resort in the Philippines, recognized for our dedication to excellence, sustainable tourism, and the creation of an idyllic escape where guests can unwind and connect with nature.

Welcome to our Resort!

The Villa Reyes resort started as a family vacation since the owner of the farm and province style wanted ambience, so they decided to make it a staycation resort with adult and kiddie pools. The Villa Reyes Resort officially opened on August 28, 2023.

Why choose us?

As a beginner and new resort we have offers and features like Free Wifi for customers, Videoke, Basketball Court, Billiards and Smart TV 55 and we make sure you make the most of your vacation at our resort.
